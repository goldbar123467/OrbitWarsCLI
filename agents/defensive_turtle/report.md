# Build Report: defensive_turtle

Template: `advanced_modular`

## What It Does

- Uses a low expansion posture.
- Uses low aggression against enemy planets.
- Reserves ships according to a high defense posture.
- Scores targets with distance, production, capture cost, and ETA terms.
- Predicts moving planets up to 60 turns ahead.

## Main Risks

- Comets are ignored.
